Aptitude Section

1. The average of 50 numbers is 38. If two numbers, 45 and 55, are added to the list, what will be the new average?

2. The average age of 20 students in a class is 15 years. When a teacher's age is included, the average age increases by 1 year. What is the age of the teacher?

3. The average weight of a group of 10 people decreases by 1 kg when a person weighing 70 kg is replaced by another person. What is the weight of the new person?

4. What is the simple interest on a sum of ₹15000 at 10% per annum for 3 years?

5. A sum of ₹5000 becomes ₹6050 in 2 years at compound interest. What is the rate of interest?

6. What is the compound interest on ₹2000 at 5% per annum for 3 years, compounded annually?

7. How many ways can 5 people be seated in a row if two particular people must always sit together?

8. In how many ways can 6 people be divided into 2 teams of 3 each?

9. How many different ways can the letters of the word "PERMUTATION" be arranged?

10. What day of the week was 1st January 2000?

11. If today is Wednesday, what will be the day of the week 50 days from today?

12. What was the day of the week on 15th August 1947?

13. A car travels 300 km in 5 hours. How long will it take to travel 450 km at the same speed?

14. Two trains are moving towards each other at speeds of 50 km/h and 60 km/h. If they are 220 km apart, how long will it take for them to meet?

15. A man walks at a speed of 6 km/h and runs at a speed of 10 km/h. If he covers 24 km in 4 hours, how much time did he spend walking?
