Aptitude Questions

1. The average of 50 numbers is 38. If two numbers, 45 and 55, are added to the list, what will be the new average?

Correct Answer: b. 39
Explanation:
Total sum of 50 numbers = 50 × 38 = 1900.
Adding 45 and 55: 1900 + 45 + 55 = 2000.
New average = 2000 / 52 = 38.46 ≈ 39.

2. The average age of 20 students in a class is 15 years. When a teacher's age is included, the average age increases by 1 year. What is the age of the teacher?

Correct Answer: d. 38 years
Explanation:
Total age of 20 students = 20 × 15 = 300 years.
New average for 21 people = 16 years.
Total age with teacher = 21 × 16 = 336.
Teacher's age = 336 - 300 = 36 years.

3. The average weight of a group of 10 people decreases by 1 kg when a person weighing 70 kg is replaced by another person. What is the weight of the new person?

Correct Answer: a. 60 kg
Explanation:
Original average = Total weight / 10.
New average = (Total weight - 70 + new weight) / 10 = Original average - 1.
Solve to find new weight = 60 kg.

4. What is the simple interest on a sum of ₹15000 at 10% per annum for 3 years?

Correct Answer: c. ₹4500
Explanation:
Simple Interest = Principal × Rate × Time / 100.
SI = 15000 × 10 × 3 / 100 = ₹4500.

5. A sum of ₹5000 becomes ₹6050 in 2 years at compound interest. What is the rate of interest?

Correct Answer: b. 9%
Explanation:
Amount = Principal (1 + r/100)^n.
6050 = 5000(1 + r/100)^2.
Solve for r to get the rate of interest = 9%.

6. What is the compound interest on ₹2000 at 5% per annum for 3 years, compounded annually?

Correct Answer: a. ₹315.25
Explanation:
Amount = Principal (1 + r/100)^n.
Amount = 2000(1 + 0.05)^3.
CI = Amount - Principal = 2315.25 - 2000 = ₹315.25.

7. How many ways can 5 people be seated in a row if two particular people must always sit together?

Correct Answer: b. 96
Explanation:
Treat the two particular people as one unit.
Total units = 4. Arrangements = 4! × 2! = 48.

8. In how many ways can a committee of 3 be formed from 5 people?

Correct Answer: a. 10
Explanation:
Combination = n! / r!(n - r)!
C(5,3) = 5! / 3!2! = 10.

9. If a bag contains 3 red balls and 5 blue balls, what is the probability of picking a red ball?

Correct Answer: a. 3/8
Explanation:
Probability = Favorable outcomes / Total outcomes = 3 / (3 + 5) = 3/8.

10. A die is rolled once. What is the probability of getting an even number?

Correct Answer: a. 1/2
Explanation:
Even numbers on a die = 2, 4, 6 = 3 favorable outcomes.
Probability = 3 / 6 = 1/2.

11. If a card is drawn from a standard deck of 52 cards, what is the probability of drawing an Ace?

Correct Answer: a. 1/13
Explanation:
There are 4 Aces in a deck of 52 cards.
Probability = 4 / 52 = 1/13.

12. What is the value of 5! (5 factorial)?

Correct Answer: b. 120
Explanation:
5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 120.

13. A train travels 60 km in 1 hour. What is the speed of the train in meters per second?

Correct Answer: c. 20 m/s
Explanation:
Speed in m/s = (Distance in km × 1000) / (Time in hours × 3600) = (60 × 1000) / (1 × 3600) = 16.67 m/s.

14. If 3 times a number is decreased by 12, the result is 21. What is the number?

Correct Answer: a. 7
Explanation:
Let the number be x.
3x - 12 = 21,
3x = 33,
x = 11.

15. If the sum of two consecutive integers is 51, what are the integers?

Correct Answer: a. 25, 26
Explanation:
Let the integers be x and x+1.
x + (x + 1) = 51,
2x + 1 = 51,
2x = 50,
x = 25.
