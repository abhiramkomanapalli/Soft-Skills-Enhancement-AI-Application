Vocabulary Questions

1. Choose the correct synonym of "Impecunious"

Correct Answer: b. Destitute
Explanation: 'Impecunious' means having little or no money; penniless.

2. Choose the correct synonym of "Lugubrious"

Correct Answer: b. Mournful
Explanation: 'Lugubrious' means looking or sounding sad and dismal.

3. Choose the correct antonym of "Ephemeral"

Correct Answer: a. Permanent
Explanation: 'Ephemeral' means lasting for a very short time; its antonym is 'permanent.'

4. Choose the correct antonym of "Truculent"

Correct Answer: c. Gentle
Explanation: 'Truculent' means eager or quick to argue or fight; its antonym is 'gentle.'

5. One who walks in sleep is called:

Correct Answer: b. Somnambulist
Explanation: A 'somnambulist' is a person who walks in their sleep.

6. A person who believes in fate is called:

Correct Answer: a. Fatalist
Explanation: A 'fatalist' is a person who believes that events are predetermined and inevitable.

7. What does the idiom "Bite the bullet" mean?

Correct Answer: a. To endure a painful experience
Explanation: The idiom 'bite the bullet' means to face a difficult situation with courage.

8. What does the idiom "Hit the sack" mean?

Correct Answer: a. To go to sleep
Explanation: The idiom 'hit the sack' means to go to bed.

9. Identify the correct sentence:

Correct Answer: b. I know the answer to this question.
Explanation: This sentence is in the present simple tense, which is correct for stating facts.

10. Choose the sentence with the correct grammar:

Correct Answer: c. She doesn't like pizza.
Explanation: This sentence is correct because it uses the proper contraction and verb form.

11. He is the ______ of the project.

Correct Answer: a. leader
Explanation: 'Leader' is the correct noun form for a person who leads a project.

12. The cat sat ______ the mat.

Correct Answer: b. on
Explanation: 'On' is the correct preposition to indicate the cat's position relative to the mat.

13. What does the phrase "Cut to the chase" mean?

Correct Answer: a. To end a conversation
Explanation: 'Cut to the chase' means to get to the point without wasting time.

14. Which of the following is a one-word substitution for "A person who writes poems"?

Correct Answer: a. Poet
Explanation: A 'poet' is a person who writes poems.

15. Fill in the blank: The teacher asked us to _____ the project by next week.

Correct Answer: a. complete
Explanation: 'Complete' is the correct form to use in this sentence.
